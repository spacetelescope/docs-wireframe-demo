Kitchen Sink Demo

Showcases every built-in action in docs-wireframe-demo.

Actions demonstrated

  1. click — #btn-sidebar, #btn-action
  2. toggle-class — #sidebar (open/close), #card-1 (highlighted)
  3. set-value — #input-search, #select-mode, #input-count
  4. set-attribute — #info-1 (data-tooltip)
  5. highlight — #card-1
  6. set-text — #card-1-tag, #status-text, #status-badge, #toast, #card-2-desc, #card-2-tag
  7. add-class — #status-badge (success), #toast (visible)
  8. remove-class — #toast (visible)
  9. scroll-into-view — #log-last
  10. remove-attribute — #info-1 (data-tooltip)
  11. pause — wait between sequences
